About PawPrints:

PawPrints is an age and family inclusive social media site dedicated to exchange of family pet photographs. It is designed in such as manner that it is effortless to navigate for both experienced and newer social media users. The content is restricted to family appropriate material intended not only to entertain viewers, but to give pet owners the opportunity to express the personality of their pets as often as they do in their own photos. We welcome all individuals young and young at heart to join our site and share with us the pictures of their fury, feathered, and fishy friends.
